About the Club

If you are looking for help getting free from alcohol and drugs, the East County Alano Club is a welcoming place where you can meet people who have been there and who are willing to offer a hand. There are no dues or fees required of those who attend and no professionals to tell you what to do. It is one alcoholic helping another.

The people who gather at the Club deal with serious problems, but not in a way that makes them always serious. If you have not laughed in a while because of your drinking and the problems that come from drinking, we have a plan to help you find your laughter again.
